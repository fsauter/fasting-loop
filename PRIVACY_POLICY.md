## 2. Information We Access, Collect, and Process

Because Fasting Progress is designed with a **Local-First Architecture**, we prioritize your privacy by keeping your data on your device.

### A. Data Stored Locally on Your Device
The App collects and stores the following information locally on your device within a secure SQLite database (Room Database):
*   **Fasting Timestamps:** The exact date and time you start and end a fasting session.
*   **Fasting Durations:** The elapsed time calculated between the start and end of your fasting sessions.
*   **Gamification and Progress Metrics:** Your current experience points (XP), accumulated levels, and current streak statistics (number of consecutive days fasted).

### B. Device and Network Permissions
To support essential system integrations, the App requests the following permissions as declared in the `AndroidManifest.xml`:
*   **Internet Access (`android.permission.INTERNET`):** Used solely to support network framework operations if required (e.g., standard platform update checks).
*   **Network State (`android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`):** Used to check if the device is connected to the internet.

**Note:** No personal fasting records or usage telemetry are transmitted over the internet.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

Any information processed by Fasting Progress is used strictly for the following purposes:
*   **Core Functionality:** To display your active fasting timer, log completed fasts, and show your historical fasting records.
*   **Gamification & Motivation:** To calculate XP, level progress, and day-of-fasting streaks to keep you motivated on your health journey.
*   **Personalization:** To display real-time physiological milestones and body stages (e.g., blood sugar drop, ketosis, autophagy transitions) corresponding to your active fast duration.

## 5. User Control, Access, and Data Deletion

In full compliance with Google Play Developer Policies and global privacy regulations, we provide you with absolute control over your personal data:

### A. Deleting Data Within the App
*   **Individual Records:** You can delete any individual completed fasting session directly from the **History Tab** by tapping the delete icon.
*   **Complete Reset:** You can delete your entire fasting history and all accumulated gamification progress at any time by tapping the **Clear All** (Trash Sweeper) icon in the **History Tab**.

### B. Deleting Data via Android System Settings
You can permanently wipe all local databases, preferences, and cached files associated with the App through your device's settings:
1.  Open your device's **Settings**.
2.  Navigate to **Apps** or **Application Manager** > **Fasting Progress**.
3.  Tap on **Storage**.
4.  Select **Clear Data** (or **Clear Storage**) and **Clear Cache**.

Once performed, all your data is permanently and irreversibly removed from your device.
